Capstone: Designing an AI Wellness Copilot

This capstone demonstrates how a student would synthesize all four course objectives into a single end-to-end AI product. Meet Aura — an AI-powered wellness copilot designed with human-centered principles.

Aura — Your Wellness Copilot

🧑‍🎓 Student Project 📋 Course 2.4 Final ⏱️ 6-Week Sprint

Aura is an AI wellness companion that helps users track mood, build healthy habits, and navigate stress — through natural conversation. The student designed every layer: from the conversational flow and copilot interaction patterns to the trust-building transparency features and graceful error handling.

🧠
Human-AI Interaction Trust calibration through confidence indicators and explainable check-in summaries.
💬
Conversational UI Multi-turn dialogue with context memory, empathetic tone, and guided prompts.
🤖
Copilot Pattern Augments user reflection rather than prescribing actions — human stays in control.
✨
UX for AI Progressive disclosure, graceful degradation, and bias-aware language design.

Aura ● Online — Wellness Copilot
Good evening! 🌙 I'm Aura, your wellness copilot. I'm here to help you reflect on your day — not to give medical advice. How are you feeling right now? Transparency: Aura states its limitations upfront
Honestly, pretty stressed. Work has been overwhelming this week.
I hear you — it sounds like work pressure has been building up. Let's unpack that a bit. On a scale of 1–10, where would you place your stress level today? Empathetic acknowledgment → guided follow-up
Probably a 7. I couldn't focus on anything after lunch.
Thanks for sharing that. I've logged your stress at 7 for today. Looking at your week, I notice a pattern: your stress tends to spike after back-to-back meetings. Would you like to explore that, or try a 3-minute breathing exercise instead?

Here's what I found: On 4 of the last 5 days where your stress was above 6, you had 3+ consecutive meetings before 2 PM. This is a correlation, not a diagnosis — but it might be worth experimenting with a meeting-free block. What do you think? Human-in-the-loop: AI suggests, human decides
